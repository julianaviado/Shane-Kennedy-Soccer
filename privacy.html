Privacy Policy

Last updated: set date before launch

Who we are. Shane Kennedy Soccer ("we," "us") provides private soccer coaching in Marin County, California. This policy explains what information this website collects and how it is used. For questions, contact dominatethebox@gmail.com or (415) 713-3029.

Information we collect. When you submit the booking form, we collect the information you enter: your name, your phone number or email address, the player's name and age, session format, current level, training goals, and preferred availability. If you contact us directly by phone or email, we receive the information you choose to share. This site does not run advertising or analytics trackers and does not set tracking cookies; your light/dark theme preference is stored only in your own browser and is never sent to us.

How we use it. We use booking information solely to respond to your inquiry, schedule and manage training sessions, and communicate with you about coaching. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information, and we do not use it for third-party marketing.

Children's privacy. Training clients are typically minors, so booking inquiries should be submitted by a parent or legal guardian. We do not knowingly collect information directly from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information without parental consent, contact us and we will delete it.

Sharing. We share information only with the service providers needed to operate this site (such as our website hosting provider, which may automatically log IP addresses and standard technical data), or if required by law. This site loads fonts and scripts from third-party content delivery networks (Google Fonts, cdnjs); those services may receive your IP address when your browser requests those files, and their own privacy policies apply.

Retention. We keep inquiry and scheduling information only as long as needed to manage training and reasonable business records, then delete it.

Your rights. You may ask what information we hold about you or your player, request a correction, or request deletion at any time by emailing dominatethebox@gmail.com. California residents may exercise these rights under the CCPA/CPRA without any difference in service.

Security. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes. We may update this policy from time to time; the current version will always be posted on this page.
